Current Chapters

Currently we have 9 chapters across the state and have over 50 registered members, although many more participate. Our chapters participate in canvassing, phonebanking, voter registration drives, and hosting discussions and events for their communities.In the past year membership has increased five fold and we could not be more excited about the current trajectory of HSDRI. We will also soon be starting a virtual chapter to allow members who do not have access to a chapter in their town or school to form a community. Check back soon for more information on the virtual chapter!
