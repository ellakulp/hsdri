What We Do

The High School Democrats of Rhode Island is the Rhode Island state chapter of the High School Democrats. We are a group of high school students dedicated to preserving democratic ideals, preparing and engaging the next generation of Rhode Islanders in politics, and advocating for issues aligned with the democratic party. We volunteer on campaigns for candidates at the local, state, and national levels, work with bills particularly important to our group, host discussions and events to engage high schoolers, and aid our communities in bipartisan volunteering efforts to help make the state better for the whole. We hope you will join us in being the next generation of Rhode Island democrats!
