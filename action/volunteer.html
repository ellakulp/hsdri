What We Do

Volunteering on a campaign, whether at the local, state, or national level, is an incredible way to have a direct impact on politics by helping to elect people with views similar to your own

Local Elections

If you are looking to volunteer for a local election (school committee, town council, mayor) it is  recommended that you visit your town hall, ask about the current candidates, and determine who you want to help get elected.

State Elections

  • Volunteer for your incumbent which you can find by clicking here
  • Volunteer for a candidate who is part of the Rhode Island Political Cooperative
    • The RI Political Cooperative hopes to elect people who will work directly for the people of our state.
