Contact Your Legislators

  1. Give your name, where you are a resident of, and that you are their constituent.
  2. Say that you are calling to let your rep/senator know of your position on  the topic.
  3. State your position and why it has a direct impact on you or your community.
  4. Ask them to take action or vote a certain way.
  5. Thank them for their time.
